title: Privacy
description: Privacy, consent, and visibility on Reliverse
---

Reliverse is designed with privacy and consent at the center.

## Consent Precedes Automation

The system does not assume. Personalization, notifications, and visibility defaults require your explicit choice. Nothing happens without your consent.

## Visibility Controls

You control who sees what:

- **Account**: Public or private, per your preference
- **Projects**: Per-project visibility settings
- **Content**: Clear, consistent rules about visibility

Settings are easy to find and change. Actions are reversible.

## Configuration is Explicit

If Reliverse personalizes tooling or recommendations, it does so based on choices you make. No hidden algorithms. No implicit profiling without consent.

## Unified Privacy Language

Privacy and visibility use consistent language across the platform. You can understand who sees your data and how it is used.

## Your Data

Reliverse is open source. You can inspect how data flows through the system. Audit trails for authentication, permissions, and sensitive actions are part of the design.
